The Engagement 04
  1. DiagnosisWK 1–2

    Characterization tests on the highest-churn, least-understood modules. You get a legibility map: what a human can still read, and what has gone write-only.

  2. StabilizationWK 3–8

    Seams, not rewrites. Boundaries around the worst parts. Real assertions under the fake suite. Change one thing without breaking three.

  3. GovernanceWK 9–16

    CLAUDE.md as the rulebook. Fitness functions in CI. A delegability rating on every module. A review rubric that catches problems before merge.

  4. TransferWK 17–24

    Your engineers own it. I go. The retainer turns into advisory or ends clean.
